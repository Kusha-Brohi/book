    Description

    When one retires they can lose their purpose in life, when one loses their soul mate as well, they start questioning their reason for life. So it was for retired ‘top-cop’ Gus Savage. Without his job or his beloved wife, he was just a sailboat without a rudder with no winds to fill his sails. With his youngest son in the federal lockup and his daughter married to a halfwit stockbroker, Gus’s future looked lonely and bleak. Then came that phone call that put him back in the lineup as a Private Detective. Now his life seemed to have meaning again with something to do, someplace to go and a client to help. This light-hearted, ‘Mystery at Paradise Cove’, helped put this Silver Fox Detective back in life’s saddle again.
